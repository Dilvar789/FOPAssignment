Recipes- World's best Scrabmled Eggs

I adopted this rope from a book called Sydney Food by Bill Ganger. Ever since tasting there eggs on my visit to Bit's restaturant in Kings cross. Sdney, IHave been after the recipe ,I have since transformed ot into what I really believe are the scrabmled eggs, I have ever tasted.

This recipe is what i call a "very Special breakfast." just look at tge ingredients to see why .I thas to br tasted to be believed.

Ingredients

The following ingredients make one serving:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of butter(10g)
  • 1/3 cup of cream(2 3/4 ounces)
  • A pinch of salt
  • Freshly miled black pepper.
  • 3 fresh chieves(chopped)

Instructions

    1. Whish eggs,cream and salt in a bowl.
    2. Melt the butter in a non stick pan over a high heat
    3. Pour eggs mixture into pan over untill it starts setting around the edge of pan.
    4. Using a wooden spatula ,bring the mixture into center as if it is omlete.
    5. Fold contents in again,leave for 20 seconds,and repeat untill the eggs are only just done.
    6. Grind a light spinkling of freshly miled pepper over the eggs.

You should only make a maximum of two serving per frying pan.
